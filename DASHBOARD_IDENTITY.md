# Wecraft: Dashboard Design & Identity Summary

## 1. Brand Identity
**Creative North Star:** *"The Artisan's Material Ledger"*  
Wecraft is a bold, utility-first management system designed for the high-intensity environment of physical workshops. It is a "material compass" that instills confidence through professional aesthetics and reliable data management.

- **Personality:** Bold, Professional, Reliable, Utility-Focused.
- **Core Goal:** To centralize order management and automate material requirements planning for artisans.

## 2. Design Principles
- **Utility-Led Hierarchy:** Critical statistics and material totals are oversized and prominent.
- **Breathable Density:** Generous whitespace and a centered max-width (1100px) prevent the interface from feeling crowded, even with dense data tables.
- **Brand Authority:** Strategic use of "Wecraft Red" to anchor attention on key actions without overwhelming the user.
- **Workshop-Ready:** High-contrast typography and large touch targets for seamless mobile use in active environments.

## 3. Visual System
### Color Palette
- **Primary:** **Wecraft Red** (`#d90f0f`) — Used for primary CTAs and active states.
- **Red Rarity Rule:** Red is limited to ≤10% of any screen to maintain its impact as a focal point.
- **Neutrals:** 
    - **Background:** Paper White (`#fafafa`)
    - **Surface/Cards:** Pure White (`#ffffff`)
    - **Text:** Ink Black (`#111111`)
    - **Muted Text:** Muted Smoke (`#777777`)
    - **Borders:** Mist Border (`#eaeaea`)

### Typography
**Primary Font:** DM Sans (Google Fonts)
**Display/Premium Font:** Plus Jakarta Sans
- **Display Stat:** 42px, Bold (800) — For "At a Glance" metrics.
- **Headline:** 18px, Bold (700) — For view titles and sections.
- **Body:** 14px, Medium (500) — For all data points and UI text.
- **Labels:** 11px, Bold (700), UPPERCASE — For navigation and table headers.

### Elevation & Shape
- **Radii:** 14px (Large) for Cards/Modals; 8px (Medium) for Buttons/Inputs.
- **Shadows:** Flat architecture with subtle Ambient Shadows (`0 2px 10px rgba(17,17,17,0.04)`) for elevation.

## 4. Key Components
- **Sidebar:** Fixed left (240px expanded / 72px collapsed). Uses a soft red active state (`#ffe5e5`).
- **Cards:** White surface with 14px corners and subtle borders.
- **Tables:** Sticky headers (`#fbfbfb`) with red tint row hovers (`rgba(217, 15, 15, 0.04)`).
- **Dossier Modal:** A premium, dark-themed production view for deep-diving into order details and material lists.
- **Bento Grid:** Asymmetrical layout used for dashboard layouts and premium analytic views.

## 5. Technical Standard
- **Responsiveness:** Highly fluid from mobile (drawer sidebar) to desktop (fixed sidebar).
- **Dark Mode:** Fully supported using a high-contrast dark palette (`#121212` background, `#1a1a1a` cards).
- **Accessibility:** WCAG AA compliant with legible typography and touch-friendly targets.
