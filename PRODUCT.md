# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Artisans and small business owners managing their production workflow. They often use the app in a busy workshop environment, needing quick access to data on both desktop and mobile.

## Product Purpose
To centralize order management and automate material requirements planning. The primary goal is to help users understand exactly what supplies (e.g., ribbons, sachets) they need to purchase or prepare to fulfill active orders, using a clear, actionable table of order details.

## Brand Personality
Bold, Professional, Reliable.

## Anti-references
- **Information Overload**: Avoid "crowded" dashboards that overwhelm the user with too many metrics at once.
- **Complexity**: No over-engineered navigation or complex color schemes that distract from the task.
- **Desktop-only**: Must not fail on mobile devices; responsiveness is critical.

## Design Principles
- **Utility-Led Clarity**: The most important data (material totals) should be the most prominent.
- **Breathable Efficiency**: Use generous whitespace and clear typography to prevent the interface from feeling "crowded," even when displaying dense tables.
- **Contextual Reliability**: The UI must feel "bold" and "professional" to instill confidence that the calculations are accurate and the business is well-managed.
- **Seamless Responsiveness**: Every feature must be as accessible on a mobile phone as it is on a desktop.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard (WCAG AA) compliance. Focus on legible typography and touch-friendly targets for mobile use in workshop settings.
